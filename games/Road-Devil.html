Road Devil V1.5

Survive the chaos. Switch weapons instantly. One mistake — you're grabbed.

A top-down survival shooter where players defend against waves of zombies using weapons and powerups.

  • Multiple zombie types (speed, normal, tank)
  • Zombie grab & escape mechanic
  • Smooth weapon switching system
