Z-Click

Fight through relentless zombie waves where every second matters — one mistake and you're grabbed.

A fast-paced top-down survival shooter focused on responsive combat, enemy variety, and tactical weapon switching.

Key Features

  • Wave-based enemy spawning system with increasing difficulty
  • Multiple zombie types with unique behaviors (normal, fast, tank)
  • Hit feedback system for satisfying combat feel
  • Enemy attack cooldown system for balanced gameplay
  • Stagger and melee attack combat

Game Details

  • Engine: Unity
  • Platform: PC / WebGL
  • Genre:Action/Horror
  • Status:Completed

Developer Notes

This project was focused on improving core combat feel and responsiveness. I worked on systems like enemy AI behavior, attack timing, and player feedback to make interactions feel impactful.
Special attention was given to weapon switching smoothness and balancing enemy difficulty across waves. The zombie grab mechanic was implemented to increase tension and punish careless movement.
Through this project, I focused on writing modular and scalable systems that can be expanded in future updates.

• Designing scalable wave-based enemy systems
• Implementing AI behaviors using state logic (chase, attack, idle)
• Creating responsive combat feedback (hit reactions, timing, delay)
• Managing player state during combat (damage, stun, grab effects)
• Structuring reusable and modular scripts in Unity
• Balancing gameplay difficulty for better player experience
• Using animation + logic together for gameplay feel
